My explanation - Why I chose the way I did

Why positions and floats?

In css there are brilliant ways of manipulating your content. Using grid-layouts and flex-box, really can save you a lot of time and effort.

These are tools I plan to use heavily in the future. However seing as this was my first solo project, I think it's important to understand how and what the different properties do.

To manipulate my content "manually", forces me to learn what properties like positions, display, left, right, top, bottom, floats are used to style your content.

Once I understand this fully, I can better utilize and understand the benefits of flex-box and grid in the future.

Why sections and divs?

There are several ways to structure your website. But in a nutshell I wanted to make sure that each page was structured with a header, body and a footer-section.

To illustrate this I mainly used the sections tag. This was just to keep things simple for myself.

In the future I plan to utilize more of the grid-layout. Using tags like article, main, header etc. to structure my website.
